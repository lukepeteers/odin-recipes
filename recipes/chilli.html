Chilli

Low effort Chilli

With this chilli you can just leave it while you do whatever you want, and to top it off the longer you leave it the better it tastes

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince
  • Mixed Beans
  • Mixed herbs
  • Passata
  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Chilli Powder
  • Leaf
  • Onion and Garlic

Method

  1. Chop onion and garlic, and put in a wok at a low-medium heat
  2. Once they are sufficently sweated. Throw the beef mince in and break apart as to mix with the Onion and Garlic
  3. Now that the mince is brown mostly, start adding the spices. Dont worry about exact measurements its hard to go wrong
  4. Add the pasata and tinned Tomatoes
  5. Throw in the mixed beans after draining
  6. Bring to a simmer and cover for half an hour
  7. After the half hour, taste the chilli and add spices as needed
  8. Leave to simmer or as long as you want, add more water if more time is needed
  9. Serve and chuck a leaf on top
