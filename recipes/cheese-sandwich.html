Ultimate Cheese Sandwich

The Cheese Sandwich to rule all Cheese Sandwich's

One Cheese Sandwich to rule them all, One Cheese Sandwich to find them, One Cheese Sandwich to bring them all, and in the darkness bind them

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice
  • Mature Cheese
  • Spreadable Cheese
  • Butter
  • Cheese and Onion crisps(Walkers)

Method

  1. Butter both sides of the bread, then spread the Spreadable Cheese on both sides of the bread
  2. Cut or grate enough mature cheese to cover the breads surface area
  3. Layer crips over the cheese graveyard
  4. Cut sandwich and become enlightened
